By making a donation, you will be supporting our efforts to create awareness, advocate for, and provide subsidized medication to individuals living with lupus disease.

Help Us Fight
Lupus Disease

At the moment, there is no cure for lupus disease. However, with proper medication, the condition can be managed effectively.

We can provide basic medications and lab tests for individuals living with lupus and other complications arising from it. Treatment differs from one individual to another depending on the manifestation.

Your donations can make a significant difference in helping us fight lupus disease.
